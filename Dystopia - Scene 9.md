# Dystopia - Scene 9
To those listening, even the elevation in volume and cadence of William's voice began to lack the emphasis required to keep there ocular slits from closing for extended periods of time.  The recording's degradation was present, but the tired ears weren't able to definitively hear it.

"The 'non-manual's' were able to effectively alter the personal database found within the gray matter.  Somehow without adding data of any formal variety. And that was only the beginning of the database restructure I was the participant in."

"After 8.71 cycles I received the next instruction set from the manual of truth"

___
The final hours of November 10th, 2096.  Wanda, absorbing a Projection.  William absorbing a Publication.  William had experienced daily muscle fatigue in his facial flanks since the secondary and tertiary absorptions of volumes that followed. 

With each new entry William found himself thinking about all the ways these citizens and places were different to his known versions of things. The ideas presented, with seemingly little to no organizational thought, were in some ways improvements. In others, the opposite was witnessed. 

But for every volume William finished and returned to the room behind the door he would find two he would choose one to return home with. Putting the unchosen one in a pile he created in the room of the moment's rejections. 

He had stopped searching the bottom of the Webby Publication for the next manual of truth instruction.  But today he didn't need to seek it.  It appeared to him with text that appeared to be swollen in relation.

*THE MANUAL OF TRUTH PROGRAM TO BEGIN EXIT PROCESS.*

The tightness in Williams food processing region, that had been absent long enough for him to file it is an unpleasant memory returned. Suddenly and with vengeance. 

He followed the link, and for a moment believed the terminal had overheated, as the screen went black. 

Then life returned, countdown timer at the top 60...59....58...

Text below it that read: "Back door of Infrastructure inc. Subfloor 6. Tomorrow 1:47 AM."

*How many subfloors can there be that I never knew about?*

___

That night progressed as usual through the established routines. With the difference being Williams inability to sleep. He passed the time but tossing this way and that but could not find the correct position to facilitate any sleep prior to rising for his meeting. 

Purple protrusions under the eyes perfectly punctuated the way William readied himself for his 2.2 mile walk. Before passing through the threshold of his sleeping quarters he turned and stared. 

Unconsciously. 

Just stood there. 

For one of only a few times within recollection William lost track of time. And didn't know just how long he stood there. 

Staring at her. 

*Must have been more than a minute. Call it 78 seconds.*

And then he exited his home. Thinking the next thing on his agenda was represented by the words swift and hasteful.
